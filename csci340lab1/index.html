Bile Titan

Threat Profile

The Bile Titan is basically a giant walking “uh-oh.” To match the gross Terminid energy, I used a public-domain close-up of larvae clustered in water.

Charger

Behavior

Chargers are armored and relentless. This public-domain beetle photo feels like “natural armor”: hard shell, tough body, and the general vibe of “don’t stand in front of it.”
