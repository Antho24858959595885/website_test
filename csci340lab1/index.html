Bile Titan • Charger • Automaton Hulk

This website is inspired by three Helldivers enemy nouns. Since the enemies themselves are copyrighted, I’m using public-domain real-life stand-ins to capture the vibe (not game screenshots).

Mission Checklist

What this page includes

  • Custom CSS colors + spacing (padding + margins)

Bile Titan

Threat Profile

A “Bile Titan” is basically a giant walking problem. For the vibe, I’m using a public-domain image of larvae clustering in water—gross, swarmy, and very “Terminid.”

Image credit: CDC / James Gathany — Public domain (U.S. federal government work). Source: Wikimedia Commons.

Charger

Behavior

Chargers are armored and relentless. This beetle photo feels like “natural armor” — it’s not a Charger from the game, but it sells the idea with a real creature.

Image credit: Peggy Greb (USDA Agricultural Research Service) — Public domain. Source: Wikimedia Commons.

Automaton Hulk

Why It’s Scary

Automatons are “metal + mission.” For a Hulk-style heavy unit vibe, I’m using a public-domain military vehicle photo to represent unstoppable mechanical force.

Image credit: U.S. Air Force photo by Tech Sgt John Houghton — Public domain (U.S. federal government work). Source: Wikimedia Commons.
