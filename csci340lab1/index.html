Bile Titan • Charger • Automaton Hulk

This page is inspired by three Helldivers enemy names. Since actual game art is copyrighted, I used public-domain/CC0 real-world images that match the vibe (not screenshots).

Bile Titan

Big Bug Energy

The Bile Titan is basically a giant walking warning. To get that huge bug feel, I used a public-domain rhinoceros/Hercules beetle photo. to me, it conveys a heavy and intimidating silhouette.

Image credit: Mr4567 — Public domain (released by copyright holder). Source: Wikimedia Commons.

Charger

Armored + Relentless

Chargers are armored and built to slam straight through your plans. This ground beetle has that “tank shell” look — thick body with a tough exterior (also sort of looks like the "Tankers" from StarShip Troopers, happy coiencidence!)

Image credit: Alejandro Santillana / Insects Unlocked (UT Austin) — CC0 1.0 (public domain dedication). Source: Wikimedia Commons.

Automaton Hulk

Heavy Unit Vibe

Automatons are metal + mission. For a heavy-unit feel, I used a public-domain military vehicle photo: big, loud, and built for going straight through problems (also loud and annoying).

Image credit: U.S. Air Force photo by Tech Sgt John Houghton — Public domain (U.S. federal government work). Source: Wikimedia Commons.
